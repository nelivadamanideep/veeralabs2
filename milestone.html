Milestones

We are pleased to announce that on May 14, 2026, Veera Labs received CBE-30 approval of its ANDA for a generic version of Cholestyramine oral suspension USP (medication primarily used to lower high blood cholesterol). This represents our first approval for this high-volume product. We are currently preparing for launch soon alongside our marketing partner, AiPing Pharmaceuticals.

According to IQVIA™, annual U.S. market sales for Cholestyramine oral suspension are estimated at approximately $200 million.
