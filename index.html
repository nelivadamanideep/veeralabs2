Pharmeceutical Research & Development Excellence

Veera Labs is a Centre of Excellence in Oral Formulation development, providing customized product development services including analytical development from our Somerset, New Jersey facility. We deliver fast and reliable prototype formulations for pharmaceutical partners.

R&D Expertise

Product development across oral and parenteral dosage forms involves a fully integrated process — from formulation design to analytical validation, stability evaluation, and nonclinical/clinical readiness.

Formulation Development

Oral solids, liquids and niche formulations includes modified-release and ODTs.

Analytical Development

Robust method development using HPLC, UHPLC, LC-MS and stability testing.
