About Veera Labs

Your reliable partner for product development services — R&D, formulation and analytical development based in Somerset, New Jersey.

Company Profile

Veera Labs is a Centre of Excellence in dosage-form R&D. Our facility in Somerset, New Jersey provides customized product development services for pharmaceutical partners, delivering lab prototypes, proof‑of‑concept trials and regulatory-ready documentation. We focus on rapid, high-quality development to support clients' timelines.

Vision & Mission

Vision

Advance pharmaceutical development through innovative formulations and analytical science that improve patient outcomes worldwide.

Mission

Deliver fast, scientifically rigorous product development and analytical support from concept to clinic with strong regulatory alignment.
