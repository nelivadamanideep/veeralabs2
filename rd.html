Research & Development

At Veera Labs, Research & Development is the foundation of our innovation and product excellence. Our experienced scientific team focuses on developing high-quality, safe, and effective pharmaceutical formulations through advanced technologies, rigorous analytical testing, and continuous process optimization. We are committed to delivering innovative healthcare solutions that meet global quality and regulatory standards.

Dosages Expertise

Veera Labs develops a broad range of dosage forms including oral solids, liquids, topicals and injectables, as well as niche and advanced delivery platforms.

Tablets

IR tablets & capsules, micro-tabs in capsules, DR/ER, ODTs, pellets, effervescent, lozenges, chewing gums, sublingual, oral thin films and sprays.

Liquids & Orals

Liquid suspensions (deflocculated), nano-emulsions, self-emulsifying micro emulsions, complexation using cyclodextrins for liquid dosages.

Topicals & Injectables

Creams, ointments, Drug loaded liposomal emulgels for enhanced skin permeability, Liposomal injections, emulsion-based injectables and peptidyl injectables.

Niche Formulations

Lipid-based drug delivery system for liquid orals and soft gelatin capsules, Bioavailability enhancing platforms, Product development of metered-dose nasal sprays.

Formulation & Analytical Development Lab

Comprehensive formulation design and analytical method development delivered with stability testing, validation, and documentation to meet regulatory expectations.
